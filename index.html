Snooker Shuffle

Step 1: Setup

🛠️ How it Works

  • 1. Setup: Decide the number of players and balls per player. Each player draws their secret coloured ball before the match starts.
  • 2. Qualification: To enter the main phase, players must pot 3 red balls. Fouls before qualifying increase the number of reds required.
  • 3. Gameplay: Once qualified, players try to secretly pot their assigned ball — while defending against others doing the same.
  • 4. Reveal: The game ends when a player pots their ball and shows the assigned number as proof.

🎯 What’s This For?

Snooker Shuffle is a web tool built to make the Shuffle game in Snooker fair, secret, and fun! 🎱 It helps players draw their secret colour ball randomly before a game and keeps things hidden until the big reveal.

This tool fits right into your game flow — just open it, set the number of players and balls, and let each player reveal their ball one at a time. No confusion, no bias, and no cheating!

🎲 How to Play the Shuffle Game

Each player draws a secret coloured ball number (2 to 7) before the match starts. These assignments are kept hidden.

  • 🔴 Qualify: To qualify for potting your colour ball, you must pot at least 3 reds. If you foul before qualifying, you must pot 1 extra red per foul.
  • 🎯 Objective: After qualifying, try to pot your assigned coloured ball. You don’t have to use a corner pocket — any legal pot counts!
  • 💥 Defense: You can prevent others from winning by potting their colour balls — but only after qualifying.
  • 👻 Dead Balls (3+ players): If your assigned ball has already been potted by someone else, you can declare it dead after potting a red and requalify to stay in the game.
  • 🏁 Win: You win by potting your ball and then revealing your assignment as proof.

Use this tool to assign balls secretly and bring extra strategy and mystery to your snooker sessions!
